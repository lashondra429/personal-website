Activities

One of my favorite activities is cooking. I first learned how to cook when I was 9 years old. As a kid, I experimented with ingredients and styles of cooking until I found what worked best for my tastes. Ever since then it’s been a life-long journey of self-discovery and epiphanies. As an adult, I have not lost my passion for cooking and have shared a few of my favorite recipes that I like to prepare.

Italian Sauteed Eggplant

Italian Sautéed Eggplants is a delicious side dish prepared with eggplants, cherry tomatoes and simple seasonings. Melanzane al funghetto (sautéed eggplant) is a typical dish from the Campania region in Italy. This dish is served mainly as an accompaniment to meat, fish or seafood recipes, although it is also common to serve it as a simple lunch with a good homemade bread.

Chocolate Hummus

Dark chocolate hummus? It’s oh-so-delicious and one of my favorite snack for parties and gatherings. Instead of vegetables like a traditional hummus, I it like a fruit dip and serve it with strawberries, pineapple, green apple slices, and my favorite: salty pretzels. It really is the best!
