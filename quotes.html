Quotes

Life has ups and downs and sometimes we need to look outside ourselves for inspiration. Whenever I need some cheering up or motivation, I like to look at some of my favorite quotes. So, I take a few breathes and after I read them, I feel better and walk away refreshed with a sense of purpose. At times we need to just take a step back and renew our minds as Romans 12:2 suggests.

“Have faith in God; Truly I tell you, if anyone says to this mountain, ‘Go, throw yourself into the sea,’ and does not doubt in their heart but believes that what they say will happen, it will be done for them.”
- Jesus
“Start by doing what's necessary; then do what's possible; and suddenly you are doing the impossible.”
- Francis of Assisi
“Darkness cannot drive out darkness: only light can do that. Hate cannot drive out hate: only love can do that.”
- Martin Luther King Jr.
“God is looking for those with whom He can do the impossible — what a pity that we plan only the things that we can do by ourselves.”
- AW Tozer
“The spirit of complaint is born out of an unwillingness to trust God with today. Like the Israelites, it means you are spending your time looking back toward Egypt or wishing for the future all the while missing what God is doing right now.”
- Priscilla Shirer
